CupPoint Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 01, 2025

CupPoint (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "the Company") respects the privacy of our users and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what measures we take to protect it.

1. Information We Collect

CupPoint may collect or access the following information:

  • Photos and Media: We only access photos that users directly select or upload.
  • Location Information: Used to display the user's current location on a map or to add location information to photos.
  • Device Information and App Usage Data (Anonymous): Information such as app version, device type, error logs, etc., for app quality improvement.

2. Purpose of Information Use

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To display photos and location data within the app
  • To improve user experience and app performance
  • To provide new features and updates

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or provide users' personal information to third parties. However, when necessary for service operation (e.g., server hosting, data analysis, etc.), we may entrust it to third-party service providers with guaranteed security.

4. Data Security

CupPoint implements appropriate technical and administrative security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. However, please be aware that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • Request to view, modify, and delete their personal information
  • Revoke photo and location permissions through device settings

If you have any requests, please contact us at vokhanhlinhdeveloper@gmail.com, and we will process them as quickly as possible.

6. Analytics Data (Anonymous Collection)

We may collect anonymous data that cannot identify individuals to understand app usage and improve quality.

7. Policy Changes

This policy may be changed, and when changed, we will notify you on this page and update the effective date.
